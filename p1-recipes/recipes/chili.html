How to make THE Chili that will keep you warm in the next Ice Age

In the unlikely event, described by Andy Weir's "Project Hail Mary", that humanity must endure a mass extinction event taking the form of a new Ice Age, we intend to bring you THE chili recipe to keep you and your loved ones warm. Here's a good place for a disclaimer: this chili will infact help you survive global temperatures dipping as low -2 celsius and you can personally hold big oil and gas companies liable if that's not the case.

Steps

  1. crack 3 large eggs in a mixing container
  2. whisk the eggs together until the consistency is nice and thick
  3. throw 1 to 2 tablespoons of salted butter on a non stick pan
  4. pour you whisked eggs into the pan
  5. after the the base layer of egg has started to solidfy, spread some Boursin cheese on top of the eggs
  6. Fresh chives, thinly sliced
  7. Potato chips, gently crushed
