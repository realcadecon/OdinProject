How to make THE Omelete from The Bear

TV's "The Bear" featured dozens of iconic cooking scenes. However, Sydney's omelete creation holds a special place in our hearts. So, in this recipe we're going to replicate that eggy goodness.

Steps:

  1. crack 3 large eggs in a mixing container
  2. whisk the eggs together until the consistency is nice and thick
  3. throw 1 to 2 tablespoons of salted butter on a non stick pan
  4. pour you whisked eggs into the pan
  5. after the the base layer of egg has started to solidfy, spread some Boursin cheese on top of the eggs
  6. Fresh chives, thinly sliced
  7. Potato chips, gently crushed
